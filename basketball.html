Buzzer Beater Basketball Stats

Looking into the 90's Bulls Dynasty and beyond, here are some of the most absurd basketball stats

Draymond Green has the highest win percentage of any player in NBA history.

From 1992-1998 Dennis Rodman led the league every year in rebounding. In the modern NBA, no one over the age of 27 has led the league in rebounding except Rodman, who started his streak at age 30. He was also only 6’7.

Wilt Chamberlain led the NBA in assists in 1967. As a center. In the 60’s.

Kobe Bryant’s MVP Season: 28.3PPG, 6.3RPG, 5.4APG, 1.8SPG / Michael Jordan’s Rookie Year: 28.2PPG, 6.5RPG, 5.9APG, 2.4SPG

In 1994, Hakeem Olajuwon won MVP, Defensive Player of the Year, the NBA finals, and finals MVP.

For five consecutive seasons, Manute Bol blocked more than 10% of all two-point shots attempted while he was on the floor.
