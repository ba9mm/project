Out of the Park Baseball Stats

From the Great Bambino to the Big Unit, these baseball facts will catch you looking.

In 2004, Barry Bonds could’ve not swung once and would still have led the league in OBP.

In 2001, Randy Johnson struck out 372 batters in 35 games. That’s an average of 10.6 a game.

In 1920, Babe Ruth hit more home runs than every team except the Phillies.

Hank Aaron would still be in the 3,000 hit club if he never hit a single home run.

In 2018, Jacob deGrom recorded a major league leading 1.70 ERA, struck out 269, and won the Cy Young award. Despite his incredible year, he was only one game above .500 (10-9).

The Oakland Athletics have played in 9 winner-take-all playoff games. They have lost every single one of them.
