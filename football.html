For the Win Football Stats

Outrageous football stats brought to us by some of the best to ever play the game

In his Junior year at Oklahoma State, Barry Sanders rushed for 2,850 yards and 44 touchdowns. That’s an average of 237.5 yards and 3.67 tds a game.

Only 7 quarterbacks have thrown for 5,000 yards once. Drew Brees has done it 5 times.

Colts kicker Adam Vinatieri’s rookie year is closer to the end of the Vietnam War than it is to current day.

In 2013, Josh Gordon had a four game stretch in which he had 774 receiving yards.

In 2009, Darelle Revis held Andre Johnson, Randy Moss, Marques Colston, Terrell Owens, Steve Smith, Roddy White, and Chad Johnson to 35 receiving yards or less.

Last season, the Seattle Seahawks went 12-4. In their 16 games combined, they outscored their opponents by only 7 points.
