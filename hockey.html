Golden Goal Hockey Stats

Highlighted by Wayne Gretzky, here are some of the wildest hockey stats.

Wayne Gretzky holds the NHL’s all time record for points, with 2,857. Second on this list is Jaromir Jagr, who has nearly 1,000 less (1,921).

Father and son duo Bobby and Brett Hull had the same points/game for their career at 1.1.

Take away every goal Gretzky ever scored, he’s still the leading scorer ever.

Boston Bruin defenseman Eddie Shore holds the records for the most face scars at 19.

In 1952, Blackhawks player Bill Mosienko scored 3 goals in 21 seconds.

In 1984, Jets player Dale Hawerchuck had 5 assists in one period.
